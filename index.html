What parts of the site did I make? I used lots of other open-source tooling, ideas, algorithms, etc. Where's the line between what I 'made' and what I just 'used'? I've followed countless tutorials, used Blender plugins, Ken Perlin's noise algorithm, etc...It's tricky to say what "I" did (Chrome Dev Tools? MacOS? Cyberspace?) vs what I 'adapted'.

That's getting pretty philosophical. Suffice to say that other than the tools above this site was created in vanilla Javascript + CSS.

The site is a work-in-progress, and a lot of optimization still needs to happen: HDR & GLB compression, lots of orphaned styles and divs, etc. The 3D wheel and the AR demo will be improved in the near future as time allows.
